About the Saudi Company for Military Industries

As the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia moves into a new phase that depends on human resources and diversification Sources of income, self-sufficiency, and fulfillment of the promise of His Royal Highness Prince Mohammed bin Salman Bin Abdulaziz, Crown Prince, Deputy Prime Minister. Defense Minister - to localize half of the spending Military By 2030, the Saudi Arabian Military Industries Corporation (SAMI) was established. In May 2017, as a national entity concerned with developing and supporting the military industries in the Kingdom and promoting self-sufficiency. IMAS will be one of the main outcomes of Vision 2030 requiring activation New sectors and opening up horizons for local investment.
